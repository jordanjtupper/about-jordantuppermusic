About Jordan

Jordan J. Tupper is a Baton Rouge-based professional musician with nearly two decades of experience performing, teaching, and leading in music. A vocalist and bass guitarist, Jordan currently performs with three Baton Rouge groups across multiple genres — classic rock, contemporary covers, and jazz.

Beyond performing, Jordan brings a background as a former band director and music educator, and serves his community as a deacon at First Presbyterian Church of Baton Rouge. He holds a Digital Marketing Certificate from Louisiana State University, blending his music career with strong communications and program-management skills.

Jordan is available for private events, weddings, corporate functions, and venue bookings throughout South Louisiana and the Gulf South.

Background

Jordan's career has spanned music education, performance, and program leadership. As a former band director, he developed instrumental programs and taught students across multiple grade levels. His professional work has also included program management, community engagement, and board service for educational and faith-based organizations.

This blend of artistic and operational experience shapes how he approaches every gig — bringing not only musicianship but also reliability, professionalism, and a clear understanding of what makes an event run smoothly.
